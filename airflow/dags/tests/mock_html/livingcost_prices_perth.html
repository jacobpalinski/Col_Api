The average cost of living in Perth is $2251, which is in the top 10% of the most expensive cities in the world, ranked 934th out of 9294 in our global list and 6th out of 64 in Australia.

The median after-tax salary is $3379, which is enough to cover living expenses for 1.5 months. Ranked 52nd (TOP 0.6%) in the list of best places to live in the world and 3rd best city to live in Australia. With an estimated population of 2.14M, Perth is the 4th largest city in Australia.
