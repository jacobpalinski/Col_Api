Because of the property prices and rent, your average apartment size (or table size in a restaurant) is typically less than half of those in the US for example. In addition, portion size of meals is also close to 30%-40% smaller.

Therefore if you want to rent the same amount of space, including public space of corridors and facilities, and have meals of the same portion size, you'd need to increase the HK numbers here by at least 60% to have "similar" effect as in the US.
