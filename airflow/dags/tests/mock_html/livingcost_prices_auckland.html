The average cost of living in Auckland is $2127, which is in the top 13% of the most expensive cities in the world, ranked 1188th out of 9294 in our global list and 2nd out of 31 in New Zealand.

The median after-tax salary is $3290, which is enough to cover living expenses for 1.5 months. Ranked 156th (TOP 2%) in the list of best places to live in the world and 1st best city to live in New Zealand. With an estimated population of 1.47M, Auckland is the 1st largest city in New Zealand.
